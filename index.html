About Me

I am an Australian-born graphic designer currently residing in San Francisco, California. I moved to the Bay Area in November 2015 to pursue the greater career prospects San Francisco has to offer and to give myself the best opportunity to learn and succeed in my field. My current role is Senior Email Designer at Williams-Sonoma, Inc for PBteen. As a designer, I my strengths lie in creativity, thinking outside the box, interactivity, and strategy. Being able to create something that is different from what else is in the world, which is also successful in its point of creation, is what gives me fulfilment and purpose in my career. To create my work I have used Photoshop, Sketch, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Protopie, Invision, HTML5 and CSS3.
